About Traffic AI Guard

Pioneering next-generation road safety through advanced Computer Vision and Deep Learning.

Our Mission

Traffic AI Guard was developed with a single goal: to significantly reduce road accidents and fatalities by enforcing critical safety regulations through automation. By leveraging state-of-the-art AI, we provide law enforcement and traffic management authorities with the tools they need to monitor vast networks of roads continuously and efficiently.

We believe that technology should serve humanity. By automating the detection of safety violations like riding without a helmet—one of the leading causes of severe motorcycle injuries—we are actively building a safer future for all commuters.

The Technology

At the core of the Traffic AI Guard system is YOLOv8 (You Only Look Once), an ultra-fast, highly accurate object detection model. Our custom-trained version of the YOLOv8-Large architecture has been finely tuned on thousands of hours of varied traffic footage to detect riders, helmets, and motorcycles under complex lighting and weather conditions.

Real-Time Inference

Processing video streams at over 30 frames per second.

High Accuracy

Achieving over 96% mAP on helmet compliance detection.

ANPR Integration

Seamlessly linking violations to vehicle registration plates.
